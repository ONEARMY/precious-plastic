100% recycled, DUH!

We’re in 2023, the world is pretty f**ked, of course these NOGGLES are made from 100% recycled, local, hand-crafted, super-awesome plastic.

Decentralized production

Recycled plastic is great but if it has to travel the world back and forth multiple times to be recycled is kind of missing the point.

‍

That’s why your NOGGLES are recycled in multiple locations trying to be as near to you as possible.
