Precious Plastic is an open source project.
That's how we work. How it comes natural.

We believe knowledge should be free.
An asset for humanity that shouldn't have a price.
We share all the outcomes of our research & development online.
For the people. For the planet.

We're open source in the sense that we share all the information,
code, drawing and source material online. For free. All our content is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution - Sharealike International 4.0. You can read a summary of that here, or check out the full license.
‍
The production of Precious Plastic solutions and knowledge
is centralised at the Precious Plastic headquarter. Hardware development is quite different from software, and we haven't found a way to fully involve everyone in the creation of knowledge yet.

If you want to contribute to the project you can help us code the community platform, get active on discord or check different ways you can support the project.
‍
Peace,
Your Precious Plastic gang 🤙
