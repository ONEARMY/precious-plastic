What data do we collect? 
Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, and any other links you list including, but not limited to, your website, social media profiles, etc)

How do we collect your data?

--Register online and place an order with any of our products and services

--Use or view our website through your browser’s cookies

How will we use your data?
-Process your order and manage your account 
-Email you with special offers on products or services
-Email you about important updates

If you agree, Stichting One Army processes your order, it may send data to, and use the resulting information from, credit reference agencies to prevent fraudulent purchases.

How do we store your data? 
Stichting One Army securely stores your data using Amazon Web Service and/or Google Cloud.

Our company will keep your personal identification information (listed above) indefinitely. Once our operations have subsided, we will delete this personal identification information.

What are your data protection rights? 
‍
Stichting One Army would like to make sure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:
‍
The right to access - You have the right to request Stichting One Army for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service. 
‍
The right to rectification - You have the right to request that Stichting One Army correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that Stichting One Army to complete information that you believe is incomplete. 
‍
The right to erasure - You have the right to request that Stichting One Army erase your personal data, under certain conditions. 
‍
The right to restrict processing - You have the right to object to Stichting One Army’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions. 
‍
The right to data portability - You have the right to request that Stichting One Army transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, directly to you, under certain conditions. 
‍
If you make a request, we have one month (30 days) to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: hello@preciousplastic.com with the subject line Privacy Policy Request 
‍
Or write to us: 29c Vestdijk 5611 Eindhoven, the Netherlands

What types of cookies do we use? 

‍There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:
Functionality - Stichting One Army uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. A mix of first - party and third party cookies are used.
