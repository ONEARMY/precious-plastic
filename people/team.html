team

For the first time in 7 years we've established a full time team of committed sapiens to maintain, develop and implement Precious Plastic globally.

Small and sweet

After a 40+ team during Version 4 we've scaled down to an hybrid team of 7-10 people dedicating their lives to push Precious Plastic.

France, for now

After having to leave our big workspace we've temporarily relocated in France where we carry out our work.

Learn how we emptied our workspace

Current team

At present we have a core team in France and the rest working remotly across Europe.
