Let's Collab

Precious Plastic collabs with forward-thinking partners to create recycling projects around the world.

How it works

Precious Plastic works with partners to maximise impact. From idea generation, guidance, building and training our team of designers, engineers and project managers make sure projects are a success.

Scoping

We work closely with partners to help them understand their needs and the type of project best suited to their context, finances and timeline.

Outcome

A detailed Project Plan

Production

We take care of everything connected with building. Machines, tools, moulds, accessories and everything needed for your recycling project will be handled by Precious Plastic.

Outcome

All machines and tools built and shipped

Set up

A team from Precious Plastic visits the final destination, receives the shipment and sets up the recycling space in collaboration with the local team.

Outcome

A fully functional recycling centre

Training

Once the space is up and running we conduct a series of workshops to train the local team on how to recycle plastic into valuable products.

Outcome

Local team trained to recycle plastic

Monitoring

Precious Plastic will be available for remote support and guidance once the project is setup.

Outcome

Remote support

latest collabs

latest

Nigeria

In collab with Moët Hennessy

Boosting local recycler Victor in Lagos, Nigeria.

In 2021 we collab'd with Moët Hennessy to equip Victor with a Sheetpress Pro and Shredder Pro to help him scale his plastic recycling business and help alleviate the plastic problem in Lagos, Nigeria.

Press kit

Jordan

In collab with rise for good

Recycling plastic with Princess Lara Faisal in Amman, Jordan.

In 2021 we collab'd with Princess Lara Faisal to create an all-women led Precious Plastic workspace in Amman.

Press kit Profile

Algeria

In collab with UNHCR

Building a recycling centre in the Sahrawi refugee camps.

In 2021 we collab'd with UNHCR to create a recycling centre in the Saharawi refugee camps and empower local refugees with the tool to transform plastic waste into valuable products.

Press kit Profile

Maldives

In collab with parley for the oceans

Turning ocean plastic into products in Male, Maldives.

In 2019 we collab'd with Parley for the Oceans to create an educational recycling workspace in a shipping container in Male, Maldives.
