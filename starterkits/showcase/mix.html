Machines

To get started in the world of Precious Plastic you can build any combination of the Basic machines.

A shredder is crucial as it is the backbone of all processes. Depending on your project you can build the Injection, Extrusion, Compression or any combination of them.

Outcome

The Mix Workspace can have multiple outcomes but generally are a great starting point for people that want to learn how plastic recycling works, the properties of plastic and Precious Plastic processes.

Once you gather the necessary experience you can also generate some income with artistic products, workshops, events and demonstrations to inspire and educate others.
