Machine

The Precious Plastic Shredder Pro is a double axis shredder that cuts plastic waste into small flakes ready for further recycling. The Shredder can shred up to 50 kg of plastic waste every hour.

Technical data
Type
Double Shaft
Cost (NL)

Blades width

Electricity

Outcome

Shredded plastic can have different sizes (small, medium, large) and can be sorted by colours to add value. The shredded plastic is sold to other Precious Plastic workspaces in the local network.
