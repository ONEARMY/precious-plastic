Machine

The Sheetpress presses shredded plastic into recycled sheets using a hydraulic press.
‍
This machine can make multiple sheets (1x1 meter) per day recycling around 20 kg of plastic per sheet.

Technical data
Type
Hydraulic press
Cost (NL)
2.500 €
Weight
400 KG
Dimensions
1620 X 1620 X 1780 MM
Pressure
10 kPa
Electricity
400V / 32AMP /1.5KW

Outcome

The Sheetpress outcome is beautiful recycled sheets of up to 1x1 meters. You can play around with colours, patterns and thicknesses and sell them, so others can design stunning products with them.
