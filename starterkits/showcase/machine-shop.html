Services

Machine shops can provide different services for their customers like assembling, welding, electrician, mould-making and machining.

To offer these services the Machine Shop might need a variety of tools and machines (welder, lathe, CNC, etc..) to build parts, moulds and machines for their customers.

Outcome

Machine Shops outcomes are parts, machines and maintenance for the Precious Plastic community.
