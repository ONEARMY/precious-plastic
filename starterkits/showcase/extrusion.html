Machine

The Extrusion Pro is a single screw machine able to extrude recycled plastic into a mould.
‍
This machine is able to recycle up to 20 kg of plastic every hour and make productions of beams, bricks and more.

Outcome

The Extrusion Workspace outcome can be beams and bricks. Once you nail your process you can run productions for your customers. Play with moulds, colours, patterns and sizes to attract different audiences.
