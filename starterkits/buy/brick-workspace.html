Starterkits

With a Brick Workspace you can create 100% recycled plastic bricks. People usually produce bricks to sell to their customers, for construction projects or cool design/art installations.

The brick

Create beautiful and colourful bricks. Write a catchy description of the bricks (rory?)
‍
*Do not use for your house :)

The Extrusion machine

The Brick Workspace Starterkit uses the Extrusion machine to recycle plastic into bricks in a continuous process that can recycle up to 200kg per day.

Learn more

The moulds

The Brick Workspace Starterkit comes with 2 moulds to create the bricks. This helps you run a continuous process and produce up to 15 bricks per hour.

Accessories

The Brick Workspace Starterkit also provides you with everything else needed to start your own workspace. Tools, furniture, racks, shelves and graphic material.

Precious Plastic expertise

Get ongoing one-to-one support, counselling and guidance straight from the Precious Plastic team. Taking you from zero to hero, the Precious Plastic way.

Requirements from your side

380v

The Extrusion machine runs on 380v, you need to make sure that your space has a three-phase power outlet.

Team

To run a Brick Workspace you need to set up a small team of 2-3 people run the multiple tasks needed.

100 sqm

We advise you to find a space at least 100 sqm to run your Brick Workspace comfotably and safely.

Shredded plastic

You need to find your own local source of recycled shredder plastic.
