Finishing

Often overlooked the finishing is one of the most important steps to make plastic products more valuable. In this video we will show you how to treat products once they are taken out of moulds. We will show you different techniques and tools to finish, products to polish and finally how to stamp your creations with the plastic type sign so people after you will be able to further recycle them.

Pro tip: Don't give our words for granted, go out and find products and techniques that fit your needs best.Don't forget to stamp your products!

Stamps

Finally, after years in our heads we managed to design and produce stamps to mark Precious Plastic products with their plastic types. If you're creating with Precious Plastic on a regular basis would be great if you would get this stamps so you can mark your plastic for further recycling. This stamps should also be understood as a kind of trademark for plastic products with a higher value.
