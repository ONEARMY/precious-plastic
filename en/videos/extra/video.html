Make a video

We want to open up the sharing process to our community so that we can learn from each other and grow our knowledge around plastic recycling. In this video we will show you how you can create a video about your technique to be shared back to the world to teach and inspire people to recycle plastic waste. We will explain what gear to use, our process of making video, creating a narrative, composition, storyboard, editing and exporting the video correctly and finally how to share it back to us so that we can add your video to our creations page.

We're super excited to learn from you and see how you are making products from plastic waste!
