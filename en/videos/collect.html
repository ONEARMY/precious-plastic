Collect

Collecting is a crucial step to run an effective plastic recycling workspace. Start collecting in the correct way without wasting time. Learn how to get plastic and set up a system to keep it coming in. Download our kit to get labels with tips and tricks to separate and collect plastic effectively.

Pro tip: Ask shops and businesses around you if they have excess plastic waste they want to get rid of, this could save you a lot of time.
