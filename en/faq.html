General

Who made this project?
Version 1.0 of this project was made by Daveh Hakkens for his graduation project at the Design Academy in 2013. For version 2.0 a few more people got involved. Kees, Alex, Mattia.
Why are you sharing it for free?
We believe this has the biggest impact on recycling. We try to make it as easy as possible for people to start.
How long have you worked on this?
Started in 2013 with V1.0 over the last years worked on Version 2.0
Do you sell your plastic objects?
No, we are not foccused on producing things from plastic. The plastic objects we created are used as an example for others to show what is possible. We would love it if you start though!

Machines

Do you sell machines?
No, we are not aiming to set up a machine production. We prefer that people build them locally or hire someone there.
Do you know someone that can build me a set?
Depends where you live, have a look at this topic to connect with someone
Can I make the machines and sell them?
yep, we would love that, the more people recycling the better. Profit is all yours my friend!
Are they safe?
If you make them properly it should be ok. Overall they are safe to work with. However a machine like the shredder can make quite some damage. But you can always customize the machines or make them more safe. Either way always watch out!

Plastic

Where do you get your plastic?
We made a video about that!
How much do you need?
Totally depends on which machine you use and what you are making. Overall the more you collect/recycle, the better
Can you use all plastics?
You can use most of the plastics out there, watch this video to learn more about it
Is it dangerous to melt?
Depends which type of plastic. The industry says if you are staying in the "melting zone temperature" you are safe. We are not sure about this, so we use good ventilation or wear a mask. If you want to know more we wrote a story about this in our download kit.
