Community Point

The Community point is a space where people, organisations and Precious Plastic spaces can connect to start recycle plastic together.

Community Points are the glue of the Precious Plastic Universe.

Strong network

The outcome of a Community Point is a strong local network. Build or grow a local network of people helping each other to save plastic from being wasted, making a living with plastic recycling and inspire others to become part of it.
