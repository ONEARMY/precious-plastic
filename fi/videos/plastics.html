Video overview

In this video we cover the basics of plastic. What it is, how much we actually use it, different types, how to recognize them, and much more. Its crucial to understand the material when you are going to work with it. Don’t worry if you don’t know anything about plastic. We've tried to make the videos super easy to follow!
