Share your creations

In this place we will share the highlights that are shared in the community. Machine customizations, things that are made with the machine, research, hacks, creations, machines that are built, etc. Everything that might inspire or help out other members. If you are into this have a look at lab.preciousplastic.com you will love it. Also make sure to share what you have made!
